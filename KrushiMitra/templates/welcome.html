About KrushiMitra

KrushiMitra is an agricultural store management system designed specifically for farmers and agricultural businesses.

Founded on April 9, 2025 by Dreamers, our mission is to simplify the management of agricultural products, sales, and inventory to help farmers focus on what they do best - farming.

With KrushiMitra, you can manage your entire agricultural store operations from a single platform, ensuring efficiency and productivity.

This project was developed under the guidance of Dr. Sayali Belhe, Assistant Professor at AISSMS IOIT.
